Festifyer Data Privacy Policy

Thank you for choosing Festifyer for your event organization needs. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This Data Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal and event-related data. By using our platform, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Information We Collect

When you create an account and organize an event on Festifyer, we collect the following types of information

Personal Information

This includes your name and email address. This information is essential for account creation and communication purposes

Event Details

We collect event-specific information such as the event name, date, location, description, and any other details you provide while setting up your event. This information is necessary for effective event management

Use of Your Information

Your data is used solely for the purpose of providing our event organization services. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal or event-related data to third parties. The only individuals who have access to your data are authorized members of our organization

Board of Directors

Our CEO, CTO, and Directors are authorized personnel who can access event data for administrative and operational purposes. This access is limited to what is necessary for ensuring the smooth functioning of our services and assisting you with any inquiries or issues.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and have implemented measures to protect your information

Encryption

All data entered by users is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols. This ensures that your data remains secure during transmission and storage.

Limited Access

Access to your event data is restricted to authorized personnel mentioned above and the event organizer. This ensures that your data is only accessible to those who require it for legitimate reasons

Data Retention

Your event data is retained for as long as your account is active and for a reasonable period thereafter. This retention is necessary for record-keeping, customer support, and legal compliance purposes. If you choose to delete your account, your event data will be permanently removed from our systems

Your Choices

Access and Updates

You have the right to access and update the personal information you have provided. You can do so by logging into your Festifyer account and making the necessary changes.

Deletion

If you decide to no longer use Festifyer, you can request the deletion of your account and associated data. Please note that this action is irreversible.

Consent

By using Festifyer and providing your personal and event data, you provide explicit consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your data as described in this Privacy Policy.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Data Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated to you through the contact information you have provided

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your data or this policy, please contact our Data Protection Officer at info@fastdev.site . We are here to assist you and address any inquiries you may have
